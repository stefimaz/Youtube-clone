Tesla Model X PLAID v Ferrari SF90 v Lambo SVJ

Welcome to the most incredible petrol v hybrid v electric drag race you’ve ever seen!

We’ve got Mat in the all-electric Tesla Model X PLAID, and he’s going up against the Ferrari SF90 and Lamborghini Aventador SVJ!

So let’s see how these three powerhouses compare. Starting with the Tesla Model X, it comes with three electric motors which combine to make an eye-watering 1,020hp and 1,020Nm of torque! But of course, this is a family-friendly SUV, so it’s not light, tipping the scales at 2,539kg!

The SF90 is much lighter, weighing in at just 1,570kg! In terms of power, it’s packing a 4-litre twin-turbo V8 coupled with three electric motors to produce an almighty 1000hp and 800Nm!

Finally, we have the Lamborghini Aventador SVJ, which amazingly is the least powerful car in this line-up, with its 6.5-litre V12 able to put down 770hp and 720Nm of torque. However, it's the lightest car in this line-up, coming in at 1,525kg.

This one is just too close to call! Can an SUV actually beat these supercars?! There’s only one way to find out… LET’S RACE!
